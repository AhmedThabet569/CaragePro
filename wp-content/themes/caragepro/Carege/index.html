About Caragepro

CaragePRO is the most exclusive detailing carwash specialists in the Kingdom of Saudi Arabia, experienced in handling the largest volume of supercars with professionalism and efficiency.

Located in the heart of Al-Khobar City, our environmentally friendly state-of-the-art facility house highly trained detailers and car washers in KSA. With our passion and touch of technical expertise, we offer our clients with trust, highest possible quality and a complete car care solution package. If you are a ‘Do-It-Yourself’ enthusiastic, we provide a high range of products to fulfill and satisfy your detailing passion.

We use modern proven-to-work technologies and highest quality car care chemicals provided by Chemical Guys, the worldwide leader for 40 years in the detailing and car care industry. Each carwash process and detailing technique is developed in-house by our specialists, starting from a comprehensive assessment of your car to providing you with a tailored best advice and optimum protection that is not available anywhere else in KSA.

Our simple philosophy drives our business to excellence: “We treat our clients the way we would like to be treated - Your car, our passion”.
