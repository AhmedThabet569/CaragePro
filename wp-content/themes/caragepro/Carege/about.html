About
CaragePRO

History

Headquartered in Al-Khobar City, CaragePRO was born out of the founder’s heart and dream in 2000 to provide Saudi Arabian’s car owners with a luxurious car care quality of detailing and premium carwash services. The founder’s interest in cars started when they worked at local car wash businesses and realized that they could provide a superior and excellent car care quality with unparallel customer service. The founder’s passion and experience in detailing flourished upon travelling around the globe, attending car care shows and visiting more than 100 detailing and premium carwash service providers and car care chemical manufacturers. The founders continued to travel and attend professional educational classes in detailing and premium carwash over a span of 20 years. In 2019 and with a strong faith, dedicated hard-work and the gain of technical expertise, the founders established a team of detailing specialists and premium carwash professionals who are hand-picked who are committed to provide luxurious car care services with extremely high standards. We are inspired to restore ‘The Luster and Fresh from The Factory’ appearance of your car, we will make sure your car will be protected, sparkle and shine for years.
In CaragePRO, we understand that it’s all about the details, if you are a Do-It-Yourself enthusiastic, we provide a high range of car care products to fulfill and satisfy your detailing passion - With a click of a mouse, we will reach your house. Get in touch with the team and obtain your free car care consultation to know which of our services are right for you.
